WHAT DATA DO WE PROCESS AND FOR HOW LONG?

We process the following categories of personal data and retain them for the specified periods:

  • Personal Identification Data (e.g., name, address): Retained for the duration of the service provision plus applicable legal limitation periods (e.g., 5 years)
  • Financial Data (e.g., payment details): Retained for the duration of the transaction plus legal limitation periods (e.g., 5–10 years)

YOUR RIGHTS

WHAT RIGHTS DO YOU HAVE OVER YOUR PERSONAL DATA?

You have the following rights under applicable data protection laws:
  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of non-essential data.
  • Right to Restriction: Limit the processing of your data in case of disputes.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a transferable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing for marketing purposes.
  • Right to Provide Post-Mortem Instructions: Specify how your data should be handled after your death.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@nomads.voyage. We may request proof of identity to verify your request.

WHO HAS ACCESS TO YOUR PERSONAL DATA?

Your data is accessible only to our teams and trusted service providers who assist in delivering our services. We ensure all service providers comply with data protection laws.

Your data is hosted on servers within the Central Asia. If transfers outside the CIS occur, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

HOW DO WE PROTECT YOUR PERSONAL DATA?

At Nomads Voyage, we implement rigorous security measures to safeguard your personal information throughout your travel journey:

  • Encryption Technology: All sensitive data (payment details, passport information) is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols during online transactions and storage.
  • Secure Storage Systems: Personal data is stored on protected servers with firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and regular security audits.
  • Limited Access Controls: Only authorized personnel with specific needs can access your information, using multi-factor authentication.
  • Payment Security: We partner with PCI-DSS compliant payment processors and never store full credit card numbers.
  • Staff Training: All employees receive regular data protection training and sign confidentiality agreements.
  • Vendor Safeguards: We carefully vet all third-party providers (hotels, tour operators) and sign data processing agreements.
  • Incident Response: We maintain a 24/7 security team and documented breach notification procedures.
  • Physical Security: Our offices and data centers have controlled access, surveillance, and secure document disposal.

For international trips, we implement additional protections when transferring data across borders, ensuring equivalent safeguards through Standard Contractual Clauses or other approved mechanisms.

While we employ industry-standard measures, no system is 100% secure. We recommend you:

  • Use strong passwords for your Nomads Voyage account
  • Avoid sharing sensitive information over unsecured channels
  • Regularly monitor your account activity

We continuously update our security practices to address emerging threats and comply with evolving regulations. For security-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@nomadsvoyage.com.

Yes, Nomads Voyage uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience on our website and services. Here's how we use them:
