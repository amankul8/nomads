Responsible Tourism with Nomads Voyage

Nomads Voyage: A Purpose-Driven Company

Nomads Voyage is a team of passionate travelers, adventurers, and nature lovers dedicated to creating meaningful journeys across the globe. For over a decade, we've been exploring remote destinations by foot, kayak, and sustainable transportation. Our love for these pristine environments compels us to protect them while sharing their beauty with responsible travelers.

As specialists in sustainable travel, we take responsibility for minimizing our environmental impact while maximizing positive social contributions. We ensure the safety of our travelers while respecting local communities and fragile ecosystems.

Since 2018, Nomads Voyage has been certified by the Kyrgyz Sustainable Tourism Council, recognizing our long-term commitment to sustainable practices across more than 150 criteria for responsible tourism.

We are proud to support meaningful change by donating a portion of our revenue to charities focused and providing care for orphaned children, helping to build brighter futures for those in need. We take direct action against poverty and support orphaned children by funding and managing our own charitable programs, ensuring help reaches those who need it most.

Our Responsible Traveler Commitment

We recognize our responsibility to protect the destinations we visit while creating positive impacts through tourism. Our Responsible Traveler Commitment outlines shared expectations between Nomads Voyage and our travelers.
Inspired by the UNWTO Global Code of Ethics for Tourism, this commitment helps preserve the natural and cultural treasures we all cherish.

Reducing Our Environmental Impact

Natural environments are our playgrounds and classrooms. We work to preserve these spaces while minimizing resource consumption through innovative solutions and responsible practices.

Biodiversity Protection

We ask our travelers to respect natural spaces and minimize disturbance to wildlife:
  • Stay on designated trails and avoid removing natural objects
  • Practice Leave No Trace principles on all trips
  • Observe wildlife from appropriate distances without feeding or approaching
  • Avoid purchasing products made from endangered species
  • Use biodegradable personal care products

Sustainable Purchasing

We partner with suppliers committed to environmental responsibility:
  • Prioritize locally-sourced, seasonal meals on all trips

Waste & Resource Management

  • Pack out non-recyclable items when local facilities are inadequate
  • Minimize single-use plastics - bring your reusable water bottle
  • Choose accommodations with responsible water practices

Carbon Reduction

  • Design trips prioritizing human-powered mobility (hiking, kayaking, cycling)
  • Offer land-based travel options when possible
  • Match trip duration to transportation impacts
  • Invest in verified carbon offset projects
  • Plant one tree for every traveler

Supporting Local Communities

Human connection is at the heart of our travels. We ensure our activities benefit local communities through:
  • Working with local partners and fairly compensating all providers
  • Developing skills and sustainable livelihoods for local guides
  • Partnering with community organizations preserving cultural heritage

We encourage respectful, meaningful exchanges between travelers and hosts:
  • Adapt your behavior to local customs during interactions
  • Approach cultural differences with curiosity and respect
  • Always ask permission before photographing people

Creating Shared Value

We design our trips to generate sustainable economic benefits for host communities:
  • Require all partners to adhere to our Supplier Code of Conduct
  • Support partners in their sustainability journey
  • Work only with legally compliant, ethical businesses
  • Ensure protection of vulnerable groups and wildlife
  • Maintain zero tolerance for corruption or unethical practices

Travelers can amplify positive impacts by:
  • Supporting local artisans by purchasing authentic crafts
  • Tipping appropriately according to local standards
  • Contributing to community projects rather than giving directly to individuals

Leave No Trace Ethos

All Nomads Voyage guides are trained in Leave No Trace principles to minimize our impact on wild places. We:
  • Pack out all waste, including micro-trash
  • Stay on durable surfaces to prevent erosion
  • Observe wildlife without disturbance
  • Use non-motorized transport whenever possible
